Work With Us

Stamen is always looking for new clients and collaborators. Yes, you should hire us!

Stamen is an ambitious and always-moving studio. We focus on high-impact projects situated at the intersection of design and technology, and work with organizations of all sizes and many kinds, from Fortune 100 companies and government agencies to individuals and museums. If you have an interesting, challenging project that you think can benefit from our involvement, please contact us, we'd love to hear from you!

From time to time we look to our community to support our efforts. If you are looking for design, internship or other work opportunities, we'd like to hear from you.
