FAQ

Have a question? Some of the most frequently asked are answered below. If you need an answer to a question that's not addressed here, please don't hesitate to get in touch.

Q. I know someone at Stamen and am trying to get in touch with them. Is info@ the best way to get in touch with them?
A. Yes.

Q. Does everyone at Stamen read these?
A. Yes.

Q. I know someone who used to work at Stamen and am trying to get in touch with them. Is info@ the best way to get in touch with them?
A. We're happy to pass your message along.

Q. Are you hiring interns?
A. Not at this time.

Q. Do you sell map prints, or prints of the Silicon Valley Bus Map?
A. Not at the moment, but we're working on it. You can satisfy your map cravings for the time being by ordering a napkin or blanket from http://softcities.net or printing your own at http://maps.stamen.com/m2i

Q. Can you answer student questions or entertain student visits?
A. Sorry, but reluctantly no, we're too busy working. We're interested in relationships with schools and universities that can be mutually beneficial, but individual or group student visits are more than we can currently handle. If you are interested in hiring us to teach at your university or school, please reach out to our Education and Outreach Manager: beth [at] stamen [dot] com.
